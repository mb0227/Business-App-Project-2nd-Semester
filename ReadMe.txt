Admins can only be through AddAdmin Form. So. Go to program.cs and replace 
Application.Run(new Homepage()); with Application.Run(new AddAdmin());
Fill in admin's info and click on add employee button. You can directly use queries too.
Now, go to program.cs and undo that change and you should be good to go.
